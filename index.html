This video game is a prototype of the CO2Monsters game, in which a humaniode plant fulfills the function of an air purifying agent which is located in the city of Paris. As the 4 scenarios progress, it will be possible to observe how nature begins to grow while the monsters of pollution (CO2) are eliminated.
